Appendix E: Equality Impact Assessment

Equality, accessibility, inclusion and AI fairness considerations for LGR architecture.

Purpose

Appendix E ensures LGR architecture decisions consider equality, accessibility, inclusion, vulnerability and digital exclusion from the start.

When an Equality Impact Assessment is required

  • Changes to resident access channels.
  • New or changed digital services.
  • Use of AI, automation or decision support.
  • Changes involving vulnerable residents or protected characteristics.
  • Changes to identity, authentication or assisted digital support.
  • Service redesign affecting statutory or high-impact services.
  • Data sharing or analytics affecting resident outcomes.
